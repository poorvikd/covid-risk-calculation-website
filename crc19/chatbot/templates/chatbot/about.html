About COVID-19

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID‑19) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2). The virus mainly transmitted through droplets generated when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or exhales. These droplets are too heavy to hang in the air, and quickly fall on floors or surfaces. You can be infected by breathing in the virus if you are within close proximity of someone who has COVID-19, or by touching a contaminated surface and then your eyes, nose or mouth.

About CRC-19

Our project is a ChatBot that can be used for COVID-19 Self-Assessment and get an approximate idea of the user’s current Health condition.

This ChatBot is a Digital Service that helps the users to self-assess, map their COVID-19 symptoms and check their Risk Score, after which a Pre-diagnostic Report is mailed to them that can be used as a first-hand report to consult a doctor.

The Bot collects basic details of the user like Name, Age, E-mail, Gender and so on. The bot then takes the real time body temperature of the user using the MLX90614 contactless temperature sensor. The Bot also gathers information about any suspected COVID-19 mild, moderate and severe symptoms shown by the user and the user’s recent travel history. Then analyzes the data to calculate the Infection Risk Score.

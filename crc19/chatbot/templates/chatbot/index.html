About COVID-19

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID‑19) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2).

SYMPTOMS

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization. Common symptoms include fever, dry cough, nasal congestion, tiredness, headache and loss of smell-taste.

How to Protect Yourself

Wear Mask

Masks or cloth face coverings should be worn in public places. Masks alone do not protect against COVID-19, and should be combined with physical distancing and hand hygiene.

Wash Your Hands

Regularly and thoroughly clean your hands with an alcohol-based hand rub or wash them with soap and water.

Stay at Home

Stay away from crowded places and avoid travelling unless required. Don’t travel in a public transport without protection.

Practise Respiratory Hygiene

Follow good respiratory hygiene. This means covering your mouth and nose with your bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze. Then dispose of the used tissue immediately.

Avoid touching face

Our hands touch many surfaces and can pick up viruses. Once contaminated, hands can transfer the virus to your eyes, nose or mouth.

Social Distancing

Maintain at least 2 metre (6 feet) distance between yourself and anyone who is coughing or sneezing.
